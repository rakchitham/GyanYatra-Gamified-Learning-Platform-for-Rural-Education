# 🚀 GyanYatra – Gamified STEM Learning Platform for Rural Schools

**GyanYatra** is a **gamified digital learning platform** designed to improve **STEM education for rural school students (Grades 6–12)**.

Built using **React.js**, the platform transforms traditional learning into **interactive game-based experiences** that increase **engagement, understanding, and retention**.

The system is optimized for **low-bandwidth environments**, supports **multilingual content**, and offers **offline-first functionality**, ensuring accessibility for students in remote areas.

---

# 🎯 Vision

To **democratize STEM education** for rural learners by combining:

- 🎮 **Gamified learning experiences**
- 🌍 **Multilingual accessibility**
- 📴 **Offline-first architecture**
- 📚 **Curriculum-aligned STEM content**

Our goal is to make **quality STEM education engaging, inclusive, and accessible** regardless of connectivity limitations.

---

# ✨ Key Features

## 🎮 Gamified Learning Experience

- Interactive **STEM-based mini-games**
- Reward system with **points, badges, and levels**
- **Leaderboards** to encourage healthy competition
- **Adaptive difficulty** based on student performance

---

## 🌍 Multilingual Learning Support

- Supports **multiple Indian languages**
- Powered by **react-i18next (i18n)**
- Language packs can be **downloaded and cached offline**
- Future-ready **audio-based learning assistance**

---

## 📴 Offline-First Architecture

Designed specifically for **low-connectivity rural environments**.

- **Service Workers** for content caching
- Works **without internet after initial load**
- Lightweight resources optimized for **low storage devices**
- Fast content access even on **slow networks**

---

## 🔬 Curriculum-Aligned STEM Modules

- Concept-focused **interactive lessons**
- Integrated **quizzes and challenges**
- **Visual simulations and experiments**
- Content mapped to **Grades 6–12 STEM curriculum**

---

## 📱 Device-Friendly Design

- Optimized for **low-RAM smartphones and tablets**
- **Minimalist UI** designed for students
- **Responsive design** for mobile and desktop devices

---

# 🛠️ Technology Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|------|------------|
| Frontend | ⚛️ React.js |
| Localization | 🌐 react-i18next |
| Offline Support | 📴 Service Workers |
